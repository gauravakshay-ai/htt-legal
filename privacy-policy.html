Privacy Policy

Healing Through Thoughts (HTT)
Last updated: April 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Healing Through Thoughts ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our content and tools, including our TikTok content posting integration.

1. Who We Are

Healing Through Thoughts is a spiritual wellness content channel operated by an individual creator based in the United Kingdom. We publish short-form and long-form video content on platforms including YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data from viewers or users directly. Our TikTok integration tool is used solely to upload content to our own TikTok creator account. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data belonging to third parties.

3. How We Use TikTok API

We use TikTok's Content Posting API exclusively to automate the uploading of our own spiritual wellness videos to our own TikTok profile. This tool does not access, read, or store any data from other TikTok users.

4. Data Storage

We do not operate a database or server that stores user data. All video content is created internally and uploaded directly via API. No third-party personal data is retained at any point.

5. Cookies

This privacy policy page does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

6. Third-Party Platforms

Our content is published on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs how your data is handled when you interact with our content on their platforms. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general adult audiences. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

8. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us directly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our YouTube channel: youtube.com/@HealingThroughThoughts
